About me

I am a professor in the Departamento de Matemática at the Universidad Nacional del Sur (Argentina). I am also a researcher at CONICET and a member of the Instituto de Matemática de Bahía Blanca.

You can learn a little bit more about me here.

Interests

  • Mathematical Physics
  • Spectral analysis of Schrödinger operators
  • Functional models of symmetric operators arising from Sturm-Liouville differential expressions

Education

  • PhD major Physics, 2002

    Virginia Polytechnic and State University (EE.UU.)

  • Licentiate in Physics, 1996

    National University of Córdoba (Argentina)
